Spring is here, and we can't think of a better way to mark the season than gathering around a table together.

## What to Expect

This is a casual, come-as-you-are potluck dinner. We'll have long tables, good lighting, and plenty of time to catch up. After the meal, we'll open the floor for brief community sharing — a chance for folks to announce upcoming projects, ask for help, or simply say something they're grateful for.

## What to Bring

- A dish to share (savory, sweet, or both)
- Reusable plates and cutlery if you have them
- A friend who hasn't been to one of our events yet

**Beverages will be provided.**

## Accessibility

The venue is wheelchair accessible with on-site parking and street parking nearby. If you have accessibility needs or dietary restrictions, please [get in touch in advance](/contact/).

## A Note on Cost

This event is free and open to everyone. A donation jar will be available for those who'd like to support future events, but there is no obligation.

We hope to see you there.
