Winter is finally loosening its grip, and we can't think of a better way to welcome the season than gathering around a table together.

## What to Expect

This is a casual, come-as-you-are potluck dinner. We'll have long tables, candles, and a small acoustic set from local musician **Petra Vásquez** starting around 6:30 PM. After dinner, we'll open the floor for brief community sharing — a chance for folks to announce upcoming projects, ask for help, or simply say something they're grateful for.

## What to Bring

- A dish to share (savory, sweet, or both — we welcome it all)
- Reusable plates and cutlery if you have them
- A friend who hasn't been to one of our events yet

**Beverages will be provided.** We'll have sparkling water, lemonade, and a couple of herbal teas.

## Accessibility

Riverside Community Hall is wheelchair accessible. There is a parking lot on site and street parking along Meadow Lane. If you have accessibility needs or dietary restrictions you'd like us to know about, please [reach out in advance](/contact/).

## A Note on Cost

This event is free and open to the community. A donation jar will be on the table for those who'd like to support future events, but there is no obligation whatsoever.

We hope to see you there. Bring your appetite and your stories — both are more than welcome.
