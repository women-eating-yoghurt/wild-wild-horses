Every community needs a place to gather — and this is ours on the web.

## Who We Are

We're a small non-profit working to bring people together around shared interests and shared values. Whether you've been part of this community for years or just discovered us today, we're glad you're here.

Our work is guided by a few simple principles:

- **Welcome** — Everyone belongs here.
- **Connect** — Relationships are at the heart of what we do.
- **Show up** — Consistently, imperfectly, together.

## What You'll Find Here

On this site you'll find news and updates from our organization, stories from the people doing the work, and a calendar of upcoming events. We'll be adding new posts regularly, so check back often or follow us on social media to stay in the loop.

## How to Get Involved

The best first step is to come to an event. Head to the [Events page](/events/) to see what's coming up. If you'd prefer to reach out first, we're always reachable through the [Contact page](/contact/).

We're glad you're here.
