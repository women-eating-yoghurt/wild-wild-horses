Spring has a way of reminding us that even the most dormant-looking ground is full of possibility. That's exactly the spirit behind Monica's Garden — and it's why we're so glad you've found your way here.

## Who We Are

Monica's Garden is a community non-profit rooted in the belief that people flourish when they have space to connect, rest, and grow alongside one another. We host events, share resources, and cultivate relationships that stretch well beyond any single gathering.

Our work is guided by three simple values:

- **Welcome** — Every person who walks through our door (virtual or literal) belongs here.
- **Nourish** — We tend to the whole person: body, mind, and spirit.
- **Grow** — We're always learning, always expanding what's possible.

## What You'll Find Here

On this site, you'll find updates from our community, stories from the people doing the work, and a calendar of upcoming events — from hands-in-the-dirt workshops to quiet evening gatherings.

We'll also be sharing resources, reflections, and the occasional recipe (because gardens and kitchens are natural neighbors).

## How to Get Involved

The best way to get started is to come to an event. Our [upcoming events page](/events/) lists everything on the horizon. If you'd rather reach out directly, our [contact page](/contact/) is always open.

We're so glad you're here. Pull up a chair — or a garden stool — and stay a while.
